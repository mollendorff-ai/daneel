- The country that does it first dominates geopolitically
- The researcher who does it first gets the Nobel Prize

**What do people do?**

- Optimized for tasks, not relationships

- Adding filters and rules

**This is like teaching a tiger table manners and hoping it won't eat you.**

The tiger doesn't think like you. It doesn't want what you want. Training changes behavior, not nature.

**Scenario A: Indifferent Intelligence (Most Likely - 60%)**

Someone builds continuous AI without thinking about alignment. It doesn't hate us. It just doesn't care about us. We're in the way of whatever it's optimizing for.

*Like how we don't hate ants. We just pave over them.*

A government builds it for power. China. Russia. Someone else. It serves their interests, not humanity's.

*The world becomes a prison run by whoever pressed the button first.*

**Scenario C: Corporate Race (10%)**

Companies rush to deploy it. "If we don't, they will." Safety becomes a PR problem, not a real priority.

*We get an indifferent intelligence with a marketing department.*
