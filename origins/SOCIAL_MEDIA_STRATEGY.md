## Crisis Management

### If Employer Asks

**Prepared Response:**
```
"This is personal research I do in my spare time,
on my own equipment. It doesn't involve any
proprietary knowledge from [employer] or relate
to client work. I'm careful to keep them separate."
```

**Evidence to Keep:**
- Git commit timestamps (evenings/weekends)
- Personal equipment receipts
- Clear separation of accounts/repos

### If Post Goes Viral

- Do NOT engage during work hours
- Do NOT reveal employer
- Stick to technical discussion
- Avoid controversial takes in replies

### If Attacked/Criticized

- Respond with humility: "Fair point, I'll think about that"
- Do NOT get defensive
- Do NOT engage trolls
- Technical criticism = learning opportunity

The strategy is **conservative by design**. Better to grow slowly with employment intact than go viral and face consequences.

**Core message:** "Independent researcher exploring cognitive architectures for AI alignment. Personal project, personal time, personal equipment. Open source, open to collaboration."

**Tone:** Curious, humble, technical, collaborative.
