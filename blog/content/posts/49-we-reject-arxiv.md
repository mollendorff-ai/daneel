## Brooks' Law

> "Adding manpower to a late software project makes it later."

Big labs have hundreds of researchers. Coordination overhead. Meeting fatigue. Committee decisions. Publish-or-perish incentives that optimize for citation count over truth.

We have three minds with shared context, ref-tools for verification, and Forge for math. No overhead. Pure velocity.

December 22: Paper published to LinkedIn, X, LessWrong.
December 23: Phase 2 goes live. First external stimuli absorbed.

That's 24 hours from publication to experimental validation.

Try that with peer review.
