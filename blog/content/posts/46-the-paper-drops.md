## The Thesis

Current alignment approaches apply values externally:
- **RLHF**: Train to be helpful (Goodhart's Law at scale)
- **Constitutional AI**: Rules in prompts (prompt injection, context limits)
- **Interpretability**: Understand the black box (pre-paradigmatic, 20% introspection accuracy)

DANEEL flips this: **Build minds whose architecture produces values naturally.**

The same way human brains develop empathy through mirror neurons and social cognition—not through training or rules, but through *structure*—DANEEL implements:

- **TMI** (Cury, 1998): Pre-linguistic thought construction via competing memory windows
- **Freudian structure**: Id/Ego/SuperEgo as functional architecture
- **Asimov's Laws**: Ethical constraints as immutable invariants ("THE BOX")
- **Russell's circumplex**: Emotions as 2D architectural components (valence × arousal)
